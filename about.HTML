About Us

CyberAmartya is an initiative focused on spreading awareness about phishing attacks and providing hands-on simulation experiences for organizations, students, and professionals.

Our goal is to build a secure digital society by educating individuals on recognizing phishing threats, understanding attacker techniques, and practicing defensive strategies.

We offer:

  • Realistic phishing simulation exercises
  • Online cybersecurity training modules
  • Educational blogs and downloadable notes
  • Certification courses (coming soon)
